## A Simple, Precise Online Stopwatch

No app download, no ads blocking the screen, no permissions to accept. This free online stopwatch starts with a single click and gives you millisecond precision with lap timing built in. Keep it open in a browser tab and use it whenever you need to track time.

## Features at a Glance

- **Millisecond display**: Time is shown as hours, minutes, seconds, and milliseconds for precise measurement
- **Lap and split tracking**: Record as many laps as you need without stopping the main timer
- **Persistent timing**: Switching tabs or minimizing the browser does not affect the elapsed time
- **Keyboard shortcuts**: Start, stop, and record laps without reaching for the mouse
- **Clean interface**: A large, readable display that works well on screens of any size

## Everyday Uses for a Stopwatch

**Exercise and workouts**: Time your runs, rest periods between sets, plank holds, or circuit training intervals. The lap feature is especially useful for tracking individual set durations within a longer session.

**Cooking**: Many recipes require precise timing for steps like boiling, searing, or resting. Open the stopwatch in a tab on your phone or laptop and time each stage without fumbling with a physical kitchen timer.

**Presentations and speeches**: Rehearse a talk with the stopwatch running to make sure you stay within your allotted time. Use laps to mark section transitions and identify parts that run long.

**Productivity techniques**: Methods like the Pomodoro Technique rely on timed work intervals. Use the stopwatch to time your focus sessions and breaks.

**Games and competitions**: Time board game turns, trivia rounds, or any friendly competition where fairness depends on equal time limits.

## Lap Timing Explained

When you press the lap button, two values are recorded:

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Lap time | Duration since the previous lap (or since start for the first lap) |
| Split time | Total elapsed time from the start |

This dual recording lets you analyze individual segments and overall performance at the same time. For example, a runner timing quarter-mile laps can see both the pace of each lap and the total race time.

## Why a Browser Stopwatch?

Your phone has a built-in stopwatch, but opening it means unlocking the device, finding the app, and navigating to the right screen. A browser stopwatch is always one click away if you already work on a computer. It also displays on a larger screen, making it easier to read from across a room during workouts or presentations.

## Keyboard Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Space | Start or stop |
| L | Record a lap |
| R | Reset |

Keyboard shortcuts make the stopwatch faster to operate, especially when your hands are busy or you need split-second timing.
