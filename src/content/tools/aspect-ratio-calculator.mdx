## Get Your Image and Video Dimensions Right Every Time

Aspect ratio determines how content looks on screens, in print, and across platforms. Getting it wrong leads to stretched images, black bars on videos, and cropped content that misses important details. Our free aspect ratio calculator helps you find the correct dimensions for any ratio, ensuring your visuals always look their best.

### 1:1 Square
- 1080 x 1080 (Instagram standard)
- 2048 x 2048 (high-res square)

## Why Aspect Ratio Matters

**Social media optimization**: Each platform has preferred dimensions. Uploading an image with the wrong aspect ratio means it will be cropped automatically, potentially cutting off important content or text.

**Responsive web design**: When building layouts that adapt to different screen sizes, maintaining correct aspect ratios prevents images and videos from appearing distorted. CSS properties like `aspect-ratio` rely on understanding these proportions.

**Video production**: Shooting or editing video at the wrong aspect ratio leads to letterboxing (black bars) or unwanted cropping when the content is displayed on standard screens.

**Print and physical media**: Posters, business cards, and photo prints all have fixed dimensions. Calculating the correct pixel dimensions at your target aspect ratio ensures nothing gets cut off at the printer.

## The Math Behind Aspect Ratios

An aspect ratio is reduced to its simplest form by dividing both width and height by their greatest common divisor (GCD). For example, 1920 x 1080 reduces to 16:9 because GCD(1920, 1080) = 120, and 1920/120 = 16 while 1080/120 = 9. This tool performs that calculation automatically for any dimensions you enter.
