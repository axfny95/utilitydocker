## Why Every Website Needs a Privacy Policy

A privacy policy is legally required if your website collects any personal data — and nearly every website does, even if only through analytics cookies or server logs. Under **GDPR** (European Union), **CCPA** (California), and similar laws worldwide, failing to disclose your data practices can result in significant fines.

## What This Generator Creates

Our privacy policy generator creates a comprehensive document covering:

- **Data collection disclosures**: What personal information you collect
- **Purpose of collection**: Why you collect this data and how you use it
- **Third-party services**: Disclosure of services like Google Analytics, Stripe, and Cloudflare
- **Cookie policy**: Information about cookies and tracking technologies
- **User rights**: GDPR data subject rights or CCPA consumer rights based on your jurisdiction
- **Children's privacy**: COPPA compliance statement
- **Contact information**: How users can reach you about privacy concerns

## Important Legal Disclaimer

This tool generates privacy policy templates for **informational purposes only**. It does not constitute legal advice. No attorney-client relationship is created by using this tool. The generated document may not cover all legal requirements specific to your jurisdiction, industry, or data practices.

**We strongly recommend consulting a licensed attorney** before publishing any privacy policy on your website. Laws vary by jurisdiction and change frequently — a legal professional can ensure your policy is complete and compliant.

## GDPR vs. CCPA: Key Differences

| Aspect | GDPR | CCPA |
|--------|------|------|
| Applies to | EU residents' data | California residents' data |
| Consent | Opt-in required | Opt-out right |
| Right to delete | Yes | Yes |
| Data portability | Yes | Limited |
| Fines | Up to 4% of global revenue | $2,500-$7,500 per violation |

## Free vs. Paid Privacy Policy Services

Many services charge $50-$200+ for privacy policy generation. Our tool provides a solid starting point for free. For businesses handling sensitive data (healthcare, finance) or operating in highly regulated industries, we recommend pairing this template with professional legal review.
