We're excited to launch UtilityDocker — a growing collection of free online tools that respect your privacy.

## What Makes Us Different

Every tool on UtilityDocker processes your data locally in your browser. When you format JSON, generate a password, or create a QR code, nothing is sent to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

## Our Launch Tools

We're starting with five essential tools:

1. **JSON Formatter & Validator** — Format, minify, and validate JSON instantly
2. **Password Generator** — Create cryptographically secure passwords
3. **Word & Character Counter** — Real-time text analysis with keyword density
4. **Privacy Policy Generator** — Create customized privacy policies for your website
5. **QR Code Generator** — Generate custom QR codes with color options

## What's Coming Next

We're adding new tools every week. Coming soon: Base64 encoder, color palette generator, image compressor, markdown editor, and many more.

Stay tuned — and if you have a tool suggestion, [let us know](/contact)!
